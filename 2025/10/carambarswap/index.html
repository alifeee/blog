THE GREAT CARAMBAR SWAP!

In 2023 I competed with my sister to swap a valueless carambar with items of increasing value to see what I could end up with. I made 9 trades in total and ended up with a jacket.

In retrospect I wish that I had stuck with the 8th trade, as I'd have a sweet headtorch from the Canadian forest fire fighting service.

This was living on an Instagram page, but now it is here, as Instagram has captured enough of my content. I present to you: the trades I made.
